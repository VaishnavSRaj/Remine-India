About Us

Remine India Private Limited is a Private incorporated on 06 December 2022. It is classified as Non-govt company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, Uttarakhand.
Our mission is to help reduce the amount of electronic waste that ends up in landfills by offering easy and responsible recycling solutions.
We believe that by recycling e-waste and Li batteries, we can make a positive impact on the environment and create a cleaner, healthier planet for future generations.
